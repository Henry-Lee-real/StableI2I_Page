Overall framework

Use this section for the high-level story of the project. This is usually the best place to explain the problem setting, why unintended changes matter in image-to-image transition, and how StableI2I provides a more reliable way to identify them.

01
Input Pair
A source image and its transformed image are given as input.
02
Target Change
The intended transition is separated from the rest of the image content.
03
Unintended Drift
Non-target changes are identified across structure and appearance.
04
StableI2I Score
A fidelity-oriented score highlights whether the transition is stable.
